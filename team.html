OUR TEAM



THE FOUNDER

To keep up with modern times and the latest marketing and allied techniques
the CAR DEALS was founded in the year 1966 at 104 Hotel Metropole Karachi,
by Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji, a perfect soft spoken, humble, modest person
standing as a pioneer in Automotive Industry. An eminent well known national
personality and leader of trading community.
He made an energetic and painstaking efforts to upgrade the CAR DEALS in to a grand
showroom initially at I.I Chundigar Road, then P.E.C.H.S now in the posh area on main
Clifton road and D.H.A Islamabad. His selfless, relentless pursuit and devotion of finance
and untiring efforts towards community upliftment and social welfare for betterment of trade
association are legendary. As the third generation of Dhanji family joined the group,
the business expanded immensely and the turnover of CAR DEALS increased many folds.

(Late) Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji

MESSAGE FROM SHABBIR HUSSAIN DHANJI

As the business entered into the second generation of CAR DEALS.
Mr. Shabbir Hussain Dhanji who after completing his Automobile Sales Promotion
course from American Motors, Detroit, USA; joined the family business and ventured into
new areas within the car trade such as direct import of luxury cars, leasing & supply to corporate
sector and multinational companies. He diversified the business towards international trading,
property and assembling of cars in Pakistan in collaboration with Chinese and American counterpart.
(Late) Shabbir Hussain Dhanji CEO

MESSAGE FROM ALI ASGHAR DHANJI

As the business entered into the third generation of CAR DEALS, Mr. Ali Asghar Dhanji who graduated from The University of Buffalo, USA. On his return did his internship with Standard Chartered Bank Karachi. He followed the footsteps of his grandfather (Late) Mr. Mohammad Hussain Dhanji and father Mr. Shabbir Hussain Dhanji, soon earned the trust and confidence of our customers who showed loyalty in him as well. Mr. Ali has an excellent knack for communicating with his clients, his clear vision, straight forward thinking and decision making has given him high respect and sound market reputation.

Like his father he is also a sportsman and have won many badminton tournaments in Pakistan and abroad. He also takes part in social activities and supports the needy in their hour of need.
ALI ASGHAR DHANJI
